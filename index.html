Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Oceandev provide?

We offer web development using Framer and Shopify, along with custom web design using Figma. You can choose a one-time project or ongoing support through our monthly plans.

What’s the difference between a one-time project and a monthly plan?

A one-time project means we design and build your site, hand it off, and you own it. A monthly retainer gives you ongoing support, updates, edits, and performance monitoring, so your site keeps growing with your business.

How long does a typical project take?

Most projects are completed in 2 to 4 weeks depending on scope and how quickly feedback comes back. E-commerce builds and larger sites with custom functionality may take 4 to 6 weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline before we start.

Will my website work on mobile and tablet?

Every site we build is fully responsive across mobile, tablet, and desktop. We test on real devices before launch so nothing breaks when a client or customer visits from their phone.

Can you redesign an existing website?

Yes. We do redesigns regularly, whether you're migrating to a new platform or just refreshing your current site. We audit what you have, identify what's hurting your conversions, and rebuild with a clear strategy.

How do I get started with a project?

The easiest way is to fill out our contact form or send us a message directly. We'll schedule a quick discovery call to understand your goals, and from there we'll put together a proposal with timeline and pricing.
